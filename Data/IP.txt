INSURANCE POLICY OVERVIEW

Insurance provides financial protection against unexpected risks.

Types of Insurance:
1. Life Insurance
2. Health Insurance
3. Motor Insurance
4. Property Insurance

Health Insurance Coverage Includes:
- Hospitalization expenses
- Pre and post hospitalization costs
- Day care procedures

Policy Terms:
- Premium must be paid regularly.
- Claims must be filed within the specified time.
- Policy exclusions are mentioned in the policy document.

Insurance claims are subject to policy terms and conditions.
